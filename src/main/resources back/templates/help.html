How to Use

Click on your state to see how it performs on our gerrymander detection tests. You can view the historical trends using the years selector above. You can also see how the tests work, or run your own with more detailed results.

On gerrymander page, you can see the results of applying our tests to Congressional districts. We’ll show you the lopsidedness and asymmetry of the results, and estimate how the state would have looked without gerrymandering. If you’re new to gerrymandering and our site, pick a state and year on the “Basic” tab. If you have more experience (or you’re more daring), you may want to explore the tests in more detail using the settings found in the “Advanced” tab.
